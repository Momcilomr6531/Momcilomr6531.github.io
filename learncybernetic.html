Cybernetics: Are We Still Alive?

Cybernetic enhancements bridge the gap between biology and technology, from bionic limbs to neural implants that enhance cognitive function. These innovations promise to restore lost abilities and expand human potential. But where do we draw the line?

Are We Redefining Humanity?

When machines become part of us, do we remain human? As we integrate technology deeper into our bodies and minds, we begin to alter the core of human experience. Cybernetics raises profound questions about identity, autonomy, and what it means to be alive.

"If our minds and bodies are editable, replaceable, and upgradable, do we lose the essence of what makes us human?"
